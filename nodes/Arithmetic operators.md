# Arithmetic operators

---
The Arithmetic operators perform the following:
- addition (`+`)
- subtraction (`-`)
- multiplication (`*`)
- exponentiation (`**`) - raising a number to a power
- division (`/`)
- modulus i.e. remainder (`%`)
- increment (`++`)
- decrement (`--`)
